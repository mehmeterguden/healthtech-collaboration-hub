9. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Question	Answer
My .edu email is not accepted. What should I do?	Ensure you are using an institutional email with a .edu or .edu.tr extension. If the issue persists, contact support.
I didn’t receive the verification email.	Check your spam/junk folder. You can also click “Resend Verification Email” on the login page.
Can I edit a published post?	Yes, you can edit your post at any time while it is in Draft or Active status.
How do I delete my account and data?	Go to Profile Settings → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
Are meetings held on the platform?	No. Meetings are conducted on external platforms (Zoom, Teams, etc.). The platform only facilitates scheduling.
